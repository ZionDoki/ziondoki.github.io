## Overview

This journal paper develops a broader verification framework for open-source software license compatibility. It extends the compliance-analysis line of work beyond a single tool into a more systematic methodology.

## Position in the research agenda

Together with LiScopeLens and related industrial collaboration, this work represents the framework-oriented side of Ziang Liu's research: moving from concrete detection techniques toward generalizable verification methods.
