## Overview

This patent extends the license-compatibility research line into a granted engineering system. It focuses on cross-language, multi-scenario analysis where interoperability structure and license propagation both affect compatibility conclusions.

Original patent title: `一种跨语言多场景的开源许可证兼容性检测系统`.

## Relationship to the archive

Together with LiScopeLens, the journal framework paper, and the Huawei collaboration project, the patent marks the applied and technology-transfer side of the open-source compliance research agenda.
