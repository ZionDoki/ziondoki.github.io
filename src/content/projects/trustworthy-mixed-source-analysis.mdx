## Project context

This ongoing research project runs from 2024 to 2027 under the National Key R&D Program of China (2024YFE0203800).

## Research focus

The project explores trustworthy analysis methods for mixed-source software components, extending Ziang Liu's work on compliance and supply chain analysis toward broader trust and assurance questions.
