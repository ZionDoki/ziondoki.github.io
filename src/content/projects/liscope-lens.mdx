## Overview

LiScopeLens is a research-driven engineering system for open-source license incompatibility analysis. It combines scope-based representation of license terms with practical workflows aimed at real repository inspection and validation.

## Impact

The tool identified and validated multiple license-compliance conflicts on the OpenHarmony main branch, supporting upstream remediation and community adoption.
